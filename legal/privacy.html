Your Rights

Comprehensive protection under GDPR and CCPA.

GDPR Data Protection

Every user is entitled to the following:

Right to Access

Request copies of your personal data (small fee may apply).

Right to Rectification

Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information.

Right to Erasure

Request deletion of your personal data.

Right to Restrict Processing

Request that we restrict the processing of your data.

Right to Object

Object to our processing of your personal data.

Right to Data Portability

Request transfer of data to another organization or to you.

CCPA Rights (California)

Under the CCPA, California consumers have the right to:

  • 1. Request disclosure of categories and specific pieces of personal data collected.
  • 2. Request deletion of personal data collected by the business.
  • 3. Opt-out of the sale of personal data.

If you wish to exercise any of these rights, please contact us. We have one month to respond to your request.

Children's Information

Witchly.Host does not knowingly collect any Personal Identifiable Information from children under the age of 13. We encourage parents to monitor online activity. If you believe your child has provided this information, please contact us immediately for removal.

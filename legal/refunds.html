Your Order Cancellation Rights

You are entitled to cancel your order within 48 hours of purchase. The deadline for cancelling an Order is strictly 48 hours from the date on which you received the Servers.

How to request a refund:

You must inform us of your decision by raising a "Ticket" on our Discord server, accessible at discord.witchly.host.

We will reimburse you no later than 14 days from the day your return is accepted. We will use the same means of payment as you used for the Order, and you will not incur any fees for such reimbursement.

Conditions for Returns

In order for the Servers to be eligible for a return, please make sure that:

  • The Servers were purchased in the last 48 hours.
  • A suitable reason is provided. A reason is declared suitable after a thorough investigation of the circumstances by our team.

The following items cannot be returned:

  • Servers made to your specifications as part of the "Custom" plan.
  • Items included as part of the "Minecraft Setup" service.
  • Servers used for illegitimate purposes or suspended for abuse.

We reserve the right to refuse returns of any merchandise that does not meet the above return conditions in our sole discretion.

Gifts and Data Deletion

If you return a server given as a "Gift" or a "Booster-perk", you will not get any reimbursement of any form.

Important: You must clear your data from the Servers within 24 hours after your return is accepted by our team. Following this period, we will be permanently deleting all your files.
